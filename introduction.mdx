Stationwise simplifies staffing for fire departments by automating scheduling, tracking time, and enhancing communication. You manage shifts, assign personnel, and ensure compliance with minimal effort. Access the platform via web or mobile to optimize your operations today.

Stationwise integrates with existing fire department systems, allowing you to pull data from CAD software or HR tools. You start by defining your stations and personnel hierarchies. The dashboard provides an overview of upcoming shifts, vacancies, and overtime projections. Use the search function to find available staff quickly during recalls.

For compliance, Stationwise tracks training certifications and ensures shifts align with labor laws. You generate audit-ready reports with one click. The platform supports multi-station departments, scaling from small volunteer units to large urban services.
